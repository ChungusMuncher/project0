Roasted Red Pepper Canapes

Moreish and creamy, your guests will be asking for more.


  • Ingredients:

  • A tiger baguette
  • A tub of cream cheese (~280g)
  • A jar of roasted red pepper antipasti
  • A small bunch of dill
  • A small bunch of chives
  • Salt and Pepper

  1. 1. Prepare the bread

    Cut the baguette into slices around an inch thick. If you'd like some extra crunch in your canapes, feel free to toast the bread under a grill for a few minutes, until it's as toasted as you'd like.

  2. 2. Prepare the cheese

    Using a sharp knife, chop the chives and dill into small pieces, making sure to only chop through once, so as to not bruise the herbs. You want all of that flavour in the cheese, not the chopping board! Reserve some of the herbs for garnishing later.
    Add the herbs, salt, and pepper to the cheese, and mix well.

  3. 3. Make the canapes!

    Spread a generous amount of herbed cream cheese on top of your bread. Pop open your jar of red peppers, and place a few strips on top. Garnish with your reserved herbs, and serve on a (clean!) chopping board or serving plate of your choice!

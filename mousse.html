Avocado Chocolate Mousse

This light and fluffy mousse makes a great end to a dinner party.


  • Ingredients:

  • A tin of full fat coconut milk (~400g), chilled overnight
  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 200g very strong dark chocolate (around 85% if possible)
  • 2 tablespoons of honey
  • 2 egg whites
  • Drop of vanilla extract

  1. 1. Prepare the mousse base

    Take your tin of coconut milk out of the fridge. All of the cream should have risen to the top of the tin. Scoop all of the cream, leaving the water in the tin, into a blender. You could use the leftover liquid for a curry or soup!
    De-stone and scoop the flesh from the avocados. Make sure they're very ripe. Add the flesh to the blender. Add your drop of vanilla extract as well.

  2. 2. Melt the chocolate

    Place a glass bowl over a pan of simmering water. Break your dark chocolate into the bowl, drizzle over the honey, and wait for it to melt. Once melted, leave to cool for a spell, then add to the coconut and avocado mixture.

  3. 3. Finish the mousse base

    Take your two egg whites, and in a separate bowl, mix them with an electric whisk until they form stiff peaks. Blend everything inside the blender into a smooth paste. Transfer the smooth mixture into a bowl, and gently fold in the egg whites.

  4. 4. Serve!

    Spoon your mixture into some nice serving glasses or bowls. Chill in the fridge for a few hours, and you're done!
